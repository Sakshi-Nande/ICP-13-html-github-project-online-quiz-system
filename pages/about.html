The Online Quiz System is a web-based application designed to conduct quizzes efficiently in a digital format. It is developed to help educational institutions, teachers, and students streamline the process of conducting and participating in quizzes.

Purpose

The primary purpose of this system is to provide a platform where quizzes can be conducted online, reducing the need for paper-based tests and manual evaluation. It saves time, increases accuracy, and provides immediate feedback to users.

Key Features

  • User Registration & Login: Secure access for students and administrators.
  • Quiz Management: Admins/teachers can create, edit, and delete quizzes easily.
  • Automatic Evaluation: Instant results and scoring after quiz submission.
  • Timer-Based Quizzes: Each quiz can have a set time limit.
  • Question Randomization: Questions appear in different orders for each user.
  • Progress Tracking: Students can view their past scores and performance.

Benefits

  • Reduces manual work and paperwork
  • Improves accuracy in scoring
  • Enables remote learning and testing
  • Accessible from anywhere, anytime

Conclusion

The Online Quiz System is a smart solution for modern education, ensuring easy assessment, fair evaluation, and a better learning experience. It combines technology with education to bring a reliable, secure, and interactive testing environment for everyone.
